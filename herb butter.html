Garlic Herb Butter Steak Recipe

A mouthwatering recipe for garlic herb butter steak that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 2 (8 oz) ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  3. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic to the melted butter and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the chopped parsley and thyme, then remove the garlic herb butter from heat.
  6. Season the steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  7. Grill the steaks for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust cooking time to your desired level of doneness.
  8. During the last minute of cooking, brush the garlic herb butter over the steaks on both sides, allowing it to melt and coat the meat.
  9. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  10. Slice the steaks against the grain and serve with any remaining garlic herb butter drizzled on top.

Enjoy your flavorful garlic herb butter steak!
