Caesar Salad Roast Chicken Recipe

A delicious and easy-to-make recipe for Caesar salad roast chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup Caesar salad dressing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels and place it in a roasting pan.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the chicken and rub it evenly to coat.
  4. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and minced garlic, rubbing the seasonings all over the bird.
  5. Place the lemon slices inside the chicken cavity.
  6. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (75째C).
  7. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before carving.
  8. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, croutons, Parmesan cheese, and Caesar dressing. Toss well to coat.
  9. Serve the roasted chicken alongside the Caesar salad.

Enjoy your delicious Caesar salad roast chicken!
