Puff Puff Recipe

A delightful recipe for making Nigerian puff puff, a sweet and fluffy fried dough snack.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, active dry yeast, and salt.
  2. Add the warm water gradually to the dry ingredients, stirring continuously until you achieve a smooth and thick batter. The batter should be thicker than pancake batter but still pourable.
  3. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let the batter rest in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size and becomes frothy.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a deep pot to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Scoop spoonfuls of the batter and carefully drop them into the hot oil using your hands or a spoon. Fry in batches to avoid overcrowding.
  6. Fry the puff puff for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Remove the fried puff puff from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve the puff puff warm and enjoy as a tasty snack!

Enjoy your delicious homemade puff puff!
