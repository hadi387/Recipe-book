Suya

Suya is a spicy and flavorful grilled meat skewer, popularly enjoyed as a street food in Nigeria.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin or chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup groundnut (peanut) powder
  • 2 tablespoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for brushing
  • Sliced onions and tomatoes for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine groundnut powder, cayenne pepper, paprika, garlic powder, ginger powder, onion powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, and salt. Mix well to create the suya spice blend.
  2. Thread the thinly sliced beef or chicken onto skewers, ensuring even distribution.
  3. Brush the meat skewers with vegetable oil on both sides.
  4. Generously sprinkle the suya spice blend over the meat, coating all sides.
  5. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the suya skewers for 3-4 minutes on each side or until cooked to your desired doneness.
  6. Remove the suya skewers from the grill and serve hot, garnished with sliced onions and tomatoes if desired.

Enjoy your delicious and spicy Suya as a snack or appetizer!
